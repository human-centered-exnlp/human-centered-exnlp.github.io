This website collects the over 200 NLP explanation studies analyzed by the paper "Explaining the Road Not Taken", CHI 2021 HCXAI Workshop.

How Did We Define an NLP Explanation Study?

We define a paper as an "NLP explanation" study by satisfying:

  • Its motivation is to explain or analyze NLP models/tasks/datasets;
  • OR it aims to develop more explainable NLP models/tasks/datasets;
  • OR the explanation format is natural language.
