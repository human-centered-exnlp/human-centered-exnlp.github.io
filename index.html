NLP Explanation Paper Definition

We define a paper as an "NLP explanation" study by satisfying:

  • (i) its motivation is to explain or analyze NLP models/tasks/datasets;
  • or (ii) it aims to develop more explainable NLP models/tasks/datasets;
  • or (iii) the explanation format is natural language.
