How Did We Define an NLP Explanation Study?

We defined a paper as an "NLP explanation" study by satisfying:

  • (i) its motivation was to explain or analyze NLP models, tasks, or datasets;
  • or (ii) it aimed to develop more explainable NLP models, tasks, or datasets;
  • or (iii) the explanation format is natural language.
